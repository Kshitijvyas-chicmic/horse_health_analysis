This is where most projects fail. You should be strict here.

You can literally send this as a checklist.

✅ REQUIRED (Non-negotiable)

True side view

Camera perpendicular to limb

No front/back angle

No rotation

Camera height

Lens roughly centered on pastern

Not from ground level

Not from above

Horse stance

Square stance

Weight evenly distributed


Limb vertical, not stepping

Full visibility

Pastern, fetlock, hoof wall, toe tip all visible

No mud, boots, wraps

Clear ground plane

Hoof touching ground

No slope

No bedding distortion

⚠️ STRONGLY RECOMMENDED

Plain background

Good lighting

Minimal shadows

Avoid white hair blending with background

Avoid extreme zoom or wide-angle distortion

❌ Images that should be rejected

Camera tilted

Horse mid-step

Device attached in some images but not others

Cropped hooves

Oblique angles

Uneven ground

❌ No wide-angle / ultra-wide lenses
❌ No cropped hooves (toe + pastern must both be fully visible)
❌ Camera distance must be ≥ 1.5 meters
